The story of the seed and the soil.

There was a tiny seed.
It lay within the earth.
Dark.
Cold.
Lonely.

The seed could sense…
a vast world above.
Light.
Air.
Life.

But it was buried in darkness.

It could stay.
Rest in the warm soil.
Safe.
No risk.

Or it could…
push through the thick layer of earth.
Painful.
Struggling.
Reaching upward.

Every seed has the potential…
to become a mighty tree.
But not every seed dares to sprout.

Like humans.
Everyone has potential.
But not everyone dares to step out of their comfort zone.

The seed cannot choose where it falls.
But it can choose…
whether to sprout or not.

You too.
You can't choose where you're born.
But you can choose…
who you become.

The soil is not the seed's enemy.
The soil nourishes.
The darkest days…
are when you grow your deepest roots.

Without the darkness of the soil…
the seed wouldn't have enough strength…
to reach up toward the sun.

The difficulties you face today…
are preparing you for a breakthrough.

When the seed sprouts…
it must endure the pain of breaking through.
The hard shell must crack.
It hurts.
But it's necessary.

You too must break through the old shell.
Limiting beliefs.
Old habits.
Fears.

To rise up.

The seed doesn't know what's above.
It doesn't know if it's strong enough.
It doesn't know if the weather will be harsh.
It only knows…
it must rise.

The seed's faith is simple.
I was born to become a tree.
And I will do that.

Have faith like the seed.
That you were born…
for a purpose.
And you will achieve it.

When the seed becomes a tree…
it doesn't forget the soil.
It grows roots deeper.
It draws nutrients from the soil.
It stands strong because of the soil.

Don't forget your roots.
Don't forget those who nurtured you.

And in the end…
every seed will one day flower and bear fruit.
It doesn't keep the fruit for itself.
It gives.
Seeds fall to the ground.
Becoming new trees again.

The cycle of life.
Give and receive.
Die to live.
Lose to gain.

You are the seed.
Life is the soil.
Don't fear the darkness.
Don't fear the pain.

Sprout.
Rise up.
And become the tree.
As you were born to become.

Because the miracle of the seed…
is not how big it grows.
But that it dared to break itself open…
to begin the journey.
