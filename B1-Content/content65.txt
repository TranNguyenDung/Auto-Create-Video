A wealth mindset from youth.

Youth is the most precious asset.

Not money.

Not degrees.

But time.

You have more time than any billionaire.

But most young people…

don't realize that.

They think getting rich is a matter for the future.

For when they are older.

For when they have experience.

But a wealth mindset…

must be formed very early.

Start with a question.

Where does money come from?

Many people think money comes from a job.

Work… get paid.

That's how an employee earns money.

Rich people don't think that way.

They think money comes from value.

The value you create for others.

The more value…

the more money.

So the first question is…

what value can you create?

What skill of yours can help others?

What problem do you solve?

The answer lies there.

Young people have one advantage…

they dare to experiment.

Nothing to lose.

No family yet.

No bank loans yet.

Failure at this stage…

is just a lesson.

Not a disaster.

So try.

Run a small business.

Sell something.

Provide a service.

Learn from the market.

The market is a harsh teacher…

but the fairest.

It will tell you…

what your value is.

One important mindset…

don't sell your time.

Time is finite.

Everyone only has 24 hours.

If you sell your time…

your income will be limited.

Rich people sell products.

Sell systems.

Sell assets.

They build something…

that can work for them…

while they sleep.

That is the difference.

Learn about investing early.

You don't need a lot of money.

Just understand the concept.

Money makes money.

Assets generate income.

Liabilities take money from your pocket.

Buy assets first.

Buy liabilities later.

A simple principle…

but few follow it.

Read books about finance.

Spend an hour each day learning.

Not learning in school.

But learning from those who have gotten rich.

Their secrets are not secret.

They are all in books.

It's just whether you are willing to read them.

And one more thing…

choose your environment.

You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with.

If you hang out with spenders.

You will spend.

If you hang out with investors.

You will invest.

Choose your friends wisely.

Youth is short.

Don't waste it on useless things.

Use your youth to build a foundation.

One day…

you will thank yourself…

for starting early.

Getting rich is not the destination.

It is a journey of the mind.

And that journey…

should begin today.
