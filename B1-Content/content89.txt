Lessons from the river.

Watch the river flow.

It begins from small streams high in the mountains.
Water crystal clear.
Pure.
Young.

Then it flows downward.
Over rocky rapids.
Waterfalls.
Curving bends.

The river never stops flowing.
It doesn't pause to regret the past.
It doesn't rush worrying about the future.
It just flows.
Forever.

The first lesson from the river…
always move forward.

Even when blocked by rocks.
Even when facing rapids and waterfalls.
The river still finds a way to continue.
Never stopping.

Life is the same.
There are always obstacles.
But you are not allowed to stop.
Find a way to keep going.
Always.

The second lesson…
the river does not fight the rocks.
It flows around them.

It doesn't try to break the boulder.
It doesn't use force to confront.
It flows around.
Gently.
Yet effectively.

There are things in life…
you don't need to fight against.
Be flexible like water.
Sometimes avoiding is also a way to win.

The third lesson…
the river flows through every terrain.

Plains.
Mountains.
Deserts.
Cities.

It adapts to every environment.
It takes in smaller streams.
It carries fertile silt.
It grows stronger.

Be like the river.
Adapt to every circumstance.
Learn from every experience.
Always grow stronger each day.

The fourth lesson…
the river sometimes flows fiercely.
Sometimes silently and calmly.
But it never stops flowing.

Your life also has ups and downs.
There are stormy days.
There are peaceful days.
But never stop moving forward.

The fifth lesson…
the river never forgets the ocean.

Its ultimate purpose is the sea.
Every drop of water…
heads toward the sea.
Not lost.
Not confused.

You too have your purpose.
Don't forget it.
No matter how many turns.
In the end…
head toward where you belong.

The sixth lesson…
the river gives without demanding.

It waters the fields.
It nourishes the trees.
It is home to countless species.
It gives without expecting anything in return.

And then water from the trees evaporates.
Rises to the sky as clouds.
Falls back down as rain.
Becomes the source for the river again.

The cycle of giving.
The more you give…
the more you receive.

The seventh lesson…
look at the river…
the water is always clean.
Because it always flows.
Still water…
breeds mosquitoes.
Breed moss.
Breed filth.

Your mind is the same.
Keep it always moving.
Always learning.
Always renewing.
Don't let your mind stagnate.

Be the river.
Flowing endlessly.
Without stopping.
Without resting.

In the end…
every river reaches the sea.
And the vast ocean…
is life itself.
Vast.
Infinite.
And always welcoming you.
