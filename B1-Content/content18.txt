The power of kindness.

In a rushing world…

kindness is the slowest thing.

But also the most powerful.

People often think…

kindness is weakness.

That it is not tough enough to survive.

But they are wrong.

Kindness is not weak.

It is a choice of the strong.

A truly strong person…

is the one who can be kind to others.

Because kindness…

is when you have the power to hurt…

but you choose not to.

Kindness does not have to be grand.

Sometimes it is just a smile.

A问候.

A helping hand.

A timely word of encouragement.

These small things…

can change someone's entire day.

Can change an entire life.

Do you know…

how a single act of kindness…

ripples outward?

When you help one person…

that person helps three others.

Those three help nine.

And so on…

kindness spreads…

like ripples on a lake.

You cannot see…

how far your action goes.

But it goes very far.

There is a touching story…

about a boy buying ice cream.

He dropped his money…

and could not pay enough.

A man quietly paid the rest.

Without saying a word.

Just smiled.

Many years later…

that man fell on hard times.

His shop was about to go bankrupt.

A stranger came…

and paid off all his debt.

That stranger was the boy from years ago.

The circles of kindness…

always come back.

Always.

Kindness has the power to heal.

When you are kind to others…

you are also healing yourself.

Because happiness does not come from receiving.

It comes from giving.

Be kind to strangers.

To loved ones.

To yourself.

Do not wait until you have the means.

You can be kind right now.

In this very moment.

A caring message.

A warm hug.

A sincere thank you.

All of these are kindness.

Do not let your kindness…

depend on how others treat you.

Be kind because that is who you are.

Not because others deserve it.

As writer Mark Twain once said…

kindness is the language…

which the deaf can hear…

and the blind can see.

In this life…

you may not be rich.

You may not be powerful.

You may not be famous.

But you can always be kind.

And kindness…

will define who you are.

Because in the end…

people will not remember how rich you were.

They will remember how you made them feel.

Leave the mark of kindness.

On every life you touch.

Because that is the most beautiful legacy…

a person can leave behind.
