The older you get… The more you understand: Not everything needs an answer.

The older you get…

the more you realize a truth that is not easy to accept.

Not everything in life…

has a clear answer.

Not every departure…

has an explanation.

Not every wound…

is healed by an apology.

And not everything that happens…

needs to be understood to its core.

When you are young…

you always want to know the reason.

You want to understand why things happened the way they did.

You want to find an answer that puts your mind at ease.

But as the years go by…

people come to understand…

life is not a math problem that always has a solution.

There are relationships that end…

without a final conversation.

There are sorrows…

that cannot be put into words.

There are things…

no matter how many years you think about them…

you still cannot find the perfect answer.

And that…

does not mean you have failed.

Sometimes…

peace does not come from understanding everything.

It comes from…

accepting that there are things…

you will never fully understand.

Growing up is not about knowing more.

It is about learning to move on…

even when some questions remain unanswered.

Because not every wound needs to be explained.

Not every loss needs a reason.

There are chapters of life…

that do not end with an answer.

They end with acceptance.

And sometimes…

that itself is a form of peace.
