The harm of sitting too much.

Did you know…

the human body isn't designed…

to sit for hours on end?

Our ancestors…

moved all day.

Hunting…

gathering…

running…

Our bodies were made for movement.

Yet…

modern life…

forces us to sit for hours in front of screens.

Sitting at a desk.

Sitting in a car.

Sitting on the sofa watching TV.

And the price we pay…

is very high.

Sitting too much compresses the spine.
The vertebrae endure constant pressure.
Back muscles gradually weaken.

Neck pain…
shoulder pain…
lower back pain…

Those dull aches…
day after day…
seeming small…
but they accumulate.

Sitting too much also affects the heart.
Blood circulates more slowly.
Fat accumulates more easily.

Studies have shown…
sitting more than six hours a day…
increases the risk of heart disease…

by up to sixty-four percent.

A frightening number.

Sitting too much also slows down metabolism.
The body doesn't burn calories efficiently.
Blood sugar rises more easily.

Diabetes…
obesity…
visceral fat…

All are linked…
to the chair you sit in every day.

But the harm doesn't stop at the physical.

Sitting too much also affects the mind.
When the body is sluggish…
the mind stagnates too.

You feel lethargic.
Lack focus.
Lack creative energy.

The brain needs good blood flow…
to function effectively.
Sitting too long reduces blood flow to the brain.

A vicious cycle begins.

The more you sit…
the more tired you are.
The more tired you are…
the more you want to sit.

But the good news is…
everything can change.

Just stand up.
Every thirty minutes…
stand up once.
Walk a few steps.
Stretch.

A very small thing…
but it can change everything.

Your body is the temple of your soul.
Don't turn it into a prison…
because of a chair.

Stand up right now.
Take a deep breath.
And remember…

health isn't everything…
but without health…
everything is meaningless.

Your legs…
were born to walk.
Don't make them sit still forever.
