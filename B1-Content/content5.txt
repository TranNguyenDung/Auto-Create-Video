Much of life's sadness… comes from expecting too much.

Human sadness…

often does not begin with reality.

It begins with expectation.

We expect others to understand us…

even though we never said a word.

We expect life to go according to plan.

We expect kindness to always be repaid with kindness.

We expect…

that everything we give…

will be returned the way we want.

But life…

does not run on anyone's expectations.

And it is the gap between what we hope for…

and what actually happens…

that creates disappointment.

Expectation is not wrong.

Everyone needs hope.

Everyone needs faith.

But when expectation becomes a rigid standard…

people become more vulnerable.

Because we no longer see things as they are.

We see everything…

through the lens of what we want it to become.

Growing up does not mean stopping hope.

It means learning to hope more wisely.

Knowing how to love…

without losing yourself.

Knowing how to be sincere…

without placing your entire happiness in someone else's hands.

Knowing how to expect…

while still preparing for the possibility that life will be different.

Expecting less…

is not about living colder.

Sometimes…

it is the way to make the soul lighter.

Because peace does not come from everything going our way.

Peace comes from…

learning to receive life…

even when it is not what we imagined.
