How to forgive yourself.

There is one person you find hardest to forgive.

Not an enemy.

Not someone who hurt you.

But yourself.

You hold your mistakes inside.

You replay your errors over and over.

You punish yourself every day…

with negative thoughts.

But if you cannot forgive yourself…

you will be stuck in the past forever.

Forgiving yourself is not giving up.

It is not saying: I was wrong, whatever.

It is acknowledging the mistake.

Learning from it.

And letting go of the burden.

The first step is to admit.

Look squarely at what you have done.

Don't deny it.

Don't justify it.

Just say: I was wrong.

That takes courage.

The second step is to understand.

Why did you do it?

Did you intend to hurt anyone?

Or were you just not清醒 enough?

Or were you just human with your limitations?

When you understand the cause…

the mistake is no longer so frightening.

The third step is to learn.

Every mistake carries a lesson.

No mistake is useless.

If you have learned something…

then that mistake had value.

Ask yourself…

what is the lesson here?

And how can I avoid repeating it?

The fourth step is to commit.

Commit to being better.

Not to atone.

But to grow.

You cannot change the past.

But you can create a new future.

The final step is to let go.

This is the hardest step.

You must decide…

to no longer carry the burden.

Imagine you are holding a large rock.

That rock is the mistake.

How heavy is it?

It's time to put it down.

You can write down your mistakes…

and burn the paper.

You can tell a trusted person…

and receive understanding.

You can look in the mirror…

and say: I forgive you.

It sounds simple.

But it takes time.

Forgiving yourself is a process.

Not an event.

Be patient with yourself.

Love yourself even when you make mistakes.

Because you deserve forgiveness.

Not because you are perfect.

But because you are trying.

Learning to forgive yourself…

is the greatest gift you can give your soul.

And when you do…

you will feel lighter.

You will be freer.

And life…

will become brighter.
