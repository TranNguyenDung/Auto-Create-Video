The benefits of drinking enough water.

There is the cheapest thing in the world…

yet the most precious for the body.

Water.

The human body…

is seventy percent water.

Blood is water.

The brain is water.

The heart is water.

The lungs are water.

Without water…

everything stops working.

But few people drink enough water each day.

They drink coffee.

They drink soda.

They drink milk tea.

But they forget…

only plain water…

truly quenches the body's thirst.

Drinking enough water makes your skin beautiful.
Your skin becomes supple and smooth.
Not dry.
Wrinkles diminish.

Water flushes out toxins through the skin.
Giving you a bright, healthy complexion.
Without expensive cosmetics.

Drinking enough water helps your brain work better.
The brain needs water to focus.
Without enough water…
you easily get headaches.
Fatigue.
Lack of focus.

Just a two percent decrease in body water…
noticeably reduces work performance.

Drinking enough water aids digestion.
The stomach needs water to digest food.
The intestines need water to absorb nutrients.
Constipation disappears…
if you drink enough water.

Every morning upon waking…
a glass of warm water…
wakes up your entire digestive system.
A small habit…
with enormous benefits.

Drinking enough water keeps your heart healthier.
Blood circulates more easily.
The heart doesn't have to overwork.
Blood pressure becomes more stable.

Drinking enough water helps your kidneys work efficiently.
The kidneys are the body's filter.
Without enough water…
the kidneys have to work harder.
Kidney stones form more easily.
Urinary tract infections occur more readily.

Drinking enough water helps you lose weight.
Water has no calories.
Water makes you feel fuller.
Water boosts metabolism.

A glass of water before a meal…
helps you eat less.
A glass of water instead of soda…
saves hundreds of calories each day.

But how should you drink correctly?

Don't wait until you're thirsty to drink.
Thirst means your body is already dehydrated.
Drink regularly.
Small sips.
Throughout the day.

Two liters each day.
One and a half to two liters.
Depending on your weight and activity.

A small tip…
keep a water bottle on your desk.
Every time you see it…
take a sip.

Every time you leave your desk…
take a sip.

Gradually…
it becomes a habit.
And your body will thank you.

Water is the source of life.
Without water…
life cannot exist.
With enough water…
life blossoms.

Drink water every day.
With gratitude.
Because every sip of water…
is a gift from nature.

Drinking enough water…
is not just taking care of your body.
It's also respecting life.
Within yourself.
