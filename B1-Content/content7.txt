How to overcome fear.

Fear…

is one of the most powerful human emotions.

Everyone has been afraid.

Afraid of failure.

Afraid of rejection.

Afraid of not being good enough.

Afraid of being judged.

Afraid of losing what you have.

But fear is not the enemy.

It is a signal.

A sign that…

you are standing before something important.

The issue is not whether you feel fear or not.

Everyone feels fear.

Brave people are not those who are unafraid.

They are those…

who, despite fear…

still move forward.

People's greatest fears…

often do not come from real things.

They come from scenarios in our heads.

Things we imagine.

Disasters that have not happened.

And most of the things we fear…

will never happen.

How to overcome fear?

First…

name it.

Do not run from it.

Do not deny it.

Look straight at the fear…

and ask yourself: what is the worst that could happen?

When you understand the worst…

you will see it is not as scary as you thought.

Second…

take action.

Fear exists…

when we stand still.

When we start moving…

it begins to fade.

You do not have to do everything at once.

Just one small step.

One small action…

moving forward.

Third…

accept that fear is part of life.

You will never be completely fearless.

And that is okay.

What matters is whether you let it stop you.

Fear is like darkness.

You cannot chase away darkness by fighting it.

You only need to light a candle…

and the darkness disappears on its own.

That candle is action.

Is faith.

Is preparation.

Is determination.

When you are brave enough to face your fear…

you will realize…

most of the fears in life…

are just walls we built ourselves.

And on the other side of that wall…

is a stronger version of yourself.

Remember…

everything you want…

lies on the other side of fear.

And the only way to get through…

is to step past it.
