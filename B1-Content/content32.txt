How to resolve conflict.

Conflict is inevitable.

Wherever there are people…

there are disagreements.

But the problem is not the conflict.

The problem is how we face it.

When conflict arises…

the first thing to do is stay calm.

Anger is like fire.

If you add fuel…

it will burn everything.

But if you know how to stop…

the fire will die on its own.

Take a deep breath.

Count from one to ten.

And ask yourself…

what truly matters here?

Often…

conflict comes from misunderstanding.

You think one way…

the other person thinks another.

And both believe they are right.

But the truth…

no one is completely right.

Nor is anyone completely wrong.

It is just different perspectives.

To resolve conflict…

first listen.

Not listening to argue.

Listen to understand.

When you truly understand…

why the other person thinks that way…

the conflict half resolves itself.

Next…

speak using "I" statements instead of "you" statements.

Do not say: you make me angry.

Say: I feel uncomfortable.

A small difference in expression…

makes a big difference in reception.

And never bring up the past.

Only talk about the present issue.

The past is over…

let it rest.

If you keep digging it up…

the wound will never heal.

Find common ground before finding differences.

Something both can agree on…

even the smallest thing.

Then build a solution from there.

Some conflicts cannot be resolved in a day.

Give each other time.

Time is the best medicine.

When you calm down…

everything becomes lighter.

Sometimes…

yielding is not weakness.

It is wisdom.

Knowing when to stop…

when to move forward.

A wise person does not need to win every argument.

They know keeping harmony is more valuable than keeping ego.

Life is too short…

to hold onto disagreements forever.

Learn to let go.

Forgiveness is not for the other person.

It is for yourself.

To lighten your heart.

To soothe your soul.

Because in the end…

human connection is what matters most.
