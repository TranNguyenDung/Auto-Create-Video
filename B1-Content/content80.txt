The power of a smile.

A smile costs nothing…

but can enrich the one who receives it.

A smile lasts only a moment…

but the memory of it can last forever.

A smile is the most wonderful gift.
That people give to one another.

When you smile…
your facial muscles activate.
They send signals to your brain.
Your brain releases endorphins.
The happiness hormone.

You smile…
you feel happier.
You feel happier…
you smile more.
A positive cycle.

Even when you don't feel happy…
just smiling…
your brain starts to feel happier.
The body can't tell the difference…
between a fake smile and a real one.

So…
smile even when times are hard.

A smile dispels stress.
When you smile…
cortisol drops.
Blood pressure drops.
Muscles relax.

All stress melts away…
with just a smile.

A smile helps you live longer.
People who smile often…
live healthier.
Live longer.
Have fewer illnesses.

A study spanning thirty-five years…
showed that people who smile often…
live an average of seven years longer.

Seven years of life…
all because of a smile.

A smile connects people.
When you smile at someone…
they can hardly help smiling back.
A smile is the universal language of humanity.
No translation needed.
No explanation needed.
Everyone understands.

A smile makes you more attractive.
People who smile often…
are more approachable.
More likeable.
More successful in relationships.

Those who smile frequently…
attract others to them.
Like sunshine attracting butterflies.

A smile helps you through hard times.
When life deals you a blow…
you can cry.
Or you can smile.
Smiling doesn't mean it doesn't hurt.
Smiling means acceptance.
And still moving forward.

A smile is the strength of the strong.
The weak hide in tears.
The strong triumph with a smile.

Smile more each day.
Smile at strangers.
Smile at colleagues.
Smile at family.
Smile at yourself in the mirror.

Don't keep your smile to yourself.
Spread it.
Because your smile…
might be the ray of light…
in someone's dark day.

You may be wealthy…
may be successful…
may be powerful…
but if you don't know how to smile…
you're missing the most precious thing.

The power of a smile…
lies not in white teeth or beautiful lips.
But in sincerity.
In the heart.
In the person within.

Smile.
No matter what life brings.
A smile can change your entire day.
And someone else's.

And the most wonderful thing…
a smile is the only thing…
the more you give…
the more you have.
