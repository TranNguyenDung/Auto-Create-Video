Quotes that change lives.

Sometimes a single quote…
can change your entire life.

A quote arriving at the right time…
like a ray of light in the darkness.
Like a gentle shock.
Like a door opening.

Here are quotes…
that have changed many people's lives.

The first quote…
"Tomorrow never comes."

You always say you'll start tomorrow.
Tomorrow you'll exercise.
Tomorrow you'll learn English.
Tomorrow you'll call your mother.

But tomorrow never comes.
There is only today.
And right now.

The second quote…
"You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with."

Look around you.
The people you associate with…
shape who you are.
If you want to change…
change the people around you.

The third quote…
"Fear is an illusion."

Real fear has only two kinds.
Fear of losing your life.
Fear of getting hurt.

Every other fear…
is an illusion.
Fear of failure.
Fear of rejection.
Fear of judgment.

It's all in your head.
Face it…
and it will dissolve.

The fourth quote…
"The best way to predict the future is to create it."

Don't wait for the future to come.
Don't hope for luck.
Take action.
Build.
Create the life you want.

The fifth quote…
"You cannot control what happens to you, but you can control how you react."

Life isn't fair.
That's the truth.
But your reaction belongs to you.
No one can take that away.

Between stimulus and response…
there is a space.
In that space…
lies your freedom and power.

The sixth quote…
"Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever."

Time is the only thing that cannot be bought back.
Don't waste it.
Live fully.
Keep learning.

The seventh quote…
"Mistakes are not failures. They are lessons."

Every time you fall…
you learn how to get back up.
Every time you fail…
you learn one more way not to do it.
Thomas Edison failed ten thousand times…
before inventing the light bulb.

He said…
I didn't fail.
I just found ten thousand ways that didn't work.

The eighth quote…
"The only thing we have to fear is fear itself."

Fear itself is the greatest enemy.
It keeps you in your comfort zone.
It stops you from soaring.
Don't be afraid. Just do it.

The ninth quote…
"Giving is the only way to have more."

This is the paradox of life.
The more you give…
the more you receive.
Love.
Joy.
Wealth.

Give first.
Without expecting anything in return.
And you will receive back manifold.

The tenth quote…
"Anything can happen. Miracles are real."

Don't lose faith.
In life.
In people.
In yourself.

A quote can change your life.
If you truly listen.
And truly act.

Pick one quote.
Write it down.
Place it where you see it every day.
And live by it.

That's when your life changes.
Truly changes.
