How to make money wisely.

Making money is a skill.

And like any other skill…

it can be learned.

It can be practiced.

Many people think…

making money is luck.

A random opportunity.

Being in the right place at the right time.

But the truth is…

making money is a consequence of value.

How much you earn…

depends on the value you bring to the world.

This is the first principle.

Of making money wisely.

Don't ask how to have a lot of money.

Ask how to create more value.

The second principle…

is to solve big problems.

Money is proportional to the problem you solve.

Solve a small problem…

you earn little.

Solve a big problem…

you earn a lot.

So find real problems.

Real pains of people.

And solve them.

The third principle…

is to build, not just trade.

Most people earn money by selling time.

One hour of work, one hour of pay.

But time is limited.

So income is also limited.

Smart people…

build systems.

Build products.

Build teams.

They earn money even when they are not working.

That is the power of assets.

An asset is something that generates money for you.

The fourth principle…

is to invest in yourself.

This is the investment with the highest return.

Nothing brings more profit.

Learn a new skill.

Read a book.

Take a course.

Expand your network.

Every investment in yourself…

increases your value.

And your earning potential.

The fifth principle…

is to know how to save and invest.

Earning a lot is not as important as keeping a lot.

Many people earn a lot…

but spend even more.

They live in a cycle.

Earn more, spend more, have nothing.

Live below your means.

Save a portion of your income.

And invest that money.

Let money work for you.

Instead of you working for money.

The final principle…

is patience.

Getting rich quick is an illusion.

Most truly wealthy people…

built their wealth over decades.

Not months.

Don't look for shortcuts.

There are no shortcuts.

Only the right path.

Making money wisely…

is not about making money fast.

It is about making money sustainably.

And building a wealthy life.

In every way.
