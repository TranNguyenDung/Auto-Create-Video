Good sleep - The foundation of health.

There is something free…

but priceless.

Sleep.

Everyone needs it.

But few treasure it.

We live in an era…

where sleeping little is seen as a virtue.

Staying up late to work.

Showing diligence.

But the truth…

lack of sleep is destroying your health.

Little by little.

Silently.

Dangerously.

Sleep is not a waste of time.

It is time for the body to repair itself.

When you sleep…

your brain is working.

It organizes memories.

Processes information.

Removes toxins.

Your body is also regenerating.

Repairing damaged cells.

Rebuilding muscles.

Balancing hormones.

Losing one night of sleep…

you can handle it.

Losing a week of sleep…

you start to feel tired.

Losing a month of sleep…

memory declines.

Poor concentration.

Mood swings.

Losing a year of sleep…

risk of heart disease increases.

Diabetes.

Obesity.

Depression.

Sleep is not a luxury.

It is a survival need.

So how do you get good sleep?

First…

respect your biological clock.

Go to bed and wake up at the same time.

Even on weekends.

Your body likes routine.

Create a pre-sleep ritual.

Turn off your phone an hour before bed.

Blue light from screens…

tricks the brain…

into thinking it is still daytime.

Instead…

read a book.

Write a journal.

Listen to soft music.

Meditate for a few minutes.

The bedroom should be a sanctuary.

Dark.

Quiet.

Cool.

The ideal temperature is around twenty degrees.

Not too hot.

Not too cold.

The body falls asleep as temperature drops.

Comfortable pillows and blankets.

A tidy space.

A cluttered room…

makes for a cluttered mind.

Avoid eating too much before bed.

A light dinner.

Finish three hours before sleeping.

Your stomach also needs rest.

And avoid coffee after noon.

Caffeine stays in your body for hours.

It is the enemy of sleep.

Exercise during the day also helps you sleep well.

But don't work out too close to bedtime.

Your body needs time to cool down.

If you cannot sleep…

don't lie tossing and turning in bed.

Get up.

Take a few steps.

Read a boring book.

When you feel sleepy, go back.

Don't let your bed become a place where you fight with sleep.

Good sleep…

is the foundation for everything.

Health.

Happiness.

Success.

Without sleep…

you cannot be the best version of yourself.

So tonight…

turn off your phone earlier.

Turn off the lights.

Breathe deeply.

And let your body drift into natural sleep.

Tomorrow morning…

you will wake up to a new day full of energy.

Because good sleep…

is a gift you give yourself.
