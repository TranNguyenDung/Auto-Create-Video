Lessons from failure.

No one likes failure.

It hurts.

It embarrasses us.

It makes us want to hide…

and never try again.

But the truth is…

there is no successful person…

who has never failed.

On the contrary…

the most successful people…

are often the ones who have failed the most.

The difference is not whether they fail or not.

It is how they face failure.

Failure is not the end.

It is data.

A feedback signal…

telling you what is not right.

What needs to change.

What needs improvement.

Every time you stumble…

you have two choices.

One is to stay where you fell…

and believe you cannot go on.

The other is to get up…

look back at what happened…

learn the lesson…

and move forward with greater wisdom.

The first lesson from failure…

is that failure does not define your worth.

You are not your failure.

You are someone who tried…

and has not yet succeeded.

That is all.

The second lesson…

failure teaches us humility.

When everything goes smoothly…

we easily think we are good.

When we fail…

we understand there is still so much to learn.

And that is where true growth begins.

The third lesson…

failure is a tough teacher…

but the most effective.

No book…

no lecture…

teaches you a deeper lesson than…

the times you personally experience failure.

The fourth lesson…

failure helps you know what you truly want.

Only when facing difficulty…

do you know if your passion is big enough.

If after failure…

you still want to continue…

then that is truly what you want to pursue.

The final lesson…

and the most important.

Failure is not the opposite of success.

It is part of success.

You cannot have one…

without experiencing the other.

Every scar is a story.

Every fall is a lesson.

And every time you get up…

you become stronger.

So…

if today you are failing…

congratulations.

You are in the best classroom of life.

Learn the lesson…

and keep moving.

Because success is not about never falling.

It is about after every fall…

you stand up stronger.
