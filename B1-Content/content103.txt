The story of the shadow and the light.

There is a story rarely told…
about the shadow and the light.

They were two friends.
Inseparable.
Yet never truly side by side.

The light always shone.
Radiant.
Dazzling.
Making everyone look up.

The shadow lingered quietly behind.
Hiding beneath objects.
Rarely noticed.
Rarely mentioned.

One day…
the shadow asked the light.

Why does everyone love you?
But me…
no one pays any attention?

The light smiled gently.
Because you don't understand your own role.

Without you…
everything would be flat and lifeless.
It's because of you…
that people see depth.
See form.
See the beauty of the world.

You give this life a third dimension.
Without anyone realizing it.

The shadow fell silent, thinking.

Perhaps…
the light was right.

Everyone is born with a role.
Some shine like the sun.
Some are quiet like the moon.

But all are necessary.

In this life…
everyone wants to be the light.
Everyone wants to be seen.
Everyone wants to be recognized.

But few accept being the shadow.
Being the one behind the scenes.
Being the one who lifts others to shine.

But ask yourself…
without darkness…
could you see the light?

Without rainy days…
would you cherish sunny days?

Without difficulties…
would you appreciate achievements?

The shadow teaches us humility.
Not everyone must stand on stage.
There are those who work silently behind the curtains.

And they are just as important.

A mother who wakes early and stays up late.
A street sweeper.
A farmer working tirelessly in the fields.

They are not famous people.
But this life needs them.

The shadow also teaches us about balance.
Too much light blinds.
Too much darkness blinds.

Life is the harmony between two extremes.
Day and night.
Joy and sadness.
Success and failure.
Light and shadow.

Accept both.
Don't only seek the light.
And forget the lessons from the darkness.

Within every person…
there is a shadow.
Those hidden corners.
The fears.
The unhealed wounds.

Don't try to erase it.
Learn to accept it.

Because the shadow is part of you.
And it has its own lessons.

In the end…
light and darkness met.
They were no longer two separate entities.

They realized…
they were two sides of the same coin.
Unable to exist without each other.
Inseparable.

And when they became one…
that was when a person found wholeness.

Between light and shadow…
is where life truly exists.

You can be the light.
You can be the shadow.
Whichever you are…
be fully present in your role.

Because both create the beauty of this life.
