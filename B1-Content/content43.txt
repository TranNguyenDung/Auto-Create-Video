Things parents should teach their children.

There are lessons not found in textbooks.

But they are more important than everything else.

They are lessons about life.

About how to be human.

And parents are the first teachers.

The first lesson…

kindness.

Teach your child to help others.

Teach your child to share.

A small act of kindness…

can change someone's entire day.

And kindness is not weakness.

It is the greatest strength.

The second lesson…

honesty.

Teach your child to tell the truth.

No matter how hard the truth may be.

An honest person…

is always respected.

Even when they are wrong.

Because honesty builds trust.

And trust is the foundation of all relationships.

The third lesson…

responsibility.

Teach your child to take responsibility for their actions.

When they do something wrong…

don't blame others.

Own the mistake.

And fix it.

Responsibility is a sign of maturity.

The fourth lesson…

perseverance.

Teach your child that no one succeeds on their first try.

Failure is a part of life.

What matters is not giving up.

Try again.

And try again.

Until you make it.

Perseverance turns the impossible into possible.

The fifth lesson…

gratitude.

Teach your child to say thank you.

To appreciate what they have.

A grateful person…

is a happy person.

They don't compare.

They don't envy.

They just thank life.

The sixth lesson…

independence.

Teach your child to do their own things.

Make their own bed.

Wash their own dishes.

Organize their own belongings.

Those small tasks…

build independence.

An independent person…

will fear nothing in life.

The seventh lesson…

respect for others.

Teach your child to respect the elderly…

respect friends…

respect the housekeeper.

Everyone deserves respect.

Regardless of wealth.

Regardless of status.

The eighth lesson…

self-love.

Teach your child that they are unique.

They don't need to be like anyone else.

They have their own value.

Love their body.

Believe in their abilities.

And don't let anyone tell them they are not good enough.

The final lesson…

follow your passion.

Teach your child to listen to their heart.

Life is too short to do what you don't love.

Find what you love.

And spend your life pursuing it.

These lessons…

cannot be taught in a day.

They are taught little by little…

through words…

through actions…

through love every day.

That is the greatest legacy parents can leave their children.
