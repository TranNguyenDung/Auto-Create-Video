How to be a good friend.

There is a beautiful saying…

"Friends are the family we choose."

But how…

does one become a good friend?

Not everyone knows.

Because friendship also needs to be learned.

Like love.

Like marriage.

First, know how to listen.

This is the most important skill.

A good friend…

is not someone who speaks well.

But someone who knows how to listen.

When your friend is going through something…

do not rush to give advice.

Do not rush to tell your own story.

Just sit there.

Listen.

Sometimes…

they do not need solutions.

They just need someone who understands.

Second, be reliable.

A good friend is someone…

who can keep a secret.

If your friend shares something private…

guard it like a treasure.

Do not turn it into a story to tell others.

Trust is hard to build…

but easy to break.

Third, show up at the right time.

You cannot always be there.

But in important moments…

try to be present.

A message when they are grieving.

A call when they are lonely.

A hug when they are weak.

These small things…

build great friendships.

Fourth, be sincere.

Friendship cannot be built on pretense.

Be yourself.

And accept your friend as they are.

Do not try to seem perfect.

Do not try to please everyone.

Sincerity is always rewarded.

With sincerity.

Fifth, know how to forgive.

Friends will sometimes hurt each other.

Maybe unintentionally.

Maybe intentionally.

Whatever the case…

learn to forgive.

If the relationship is worth it…

give it a second chance.

But also know when to stop.

If a friendship only brings pain…

have the courage to let go.

Sixth, be supportive.

A good friend…

does not envy your success.

But celebrates with you.

Does not pull you down when you rise.

But pushes you higher.

Be a cheerleader for each other's dreams.

Instead of saying "you cannot do it"…

say "you can do it".

And finally…

be patient.

Everyone has their own pace.

Their own problems.

Their own battles.

Sometimes your friend will be distant.

Sometimes they will not reply to messages.

Sometimes they will forget your birthday.

But if the friendship is real…

it will survive all.

Be patient.

And always keep your arms open.

Because friendship is not a race.

It is a journey.

Walking through life together.

Laughing together, crying together.

Growing old together.

And looking back together…

on the memories made.

That is the most precious gift.

Life has to offer.

Treasure your true friends.

And be a good friend yourself.

Because to have a good friend…

first you must be a good friend.
