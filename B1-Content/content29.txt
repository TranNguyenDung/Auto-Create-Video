How to behave wisely in society.

Behavior is an art.

An art no one teaches you in school.

But it determines…

whether you are loved or hated.

Whether you are respected or looked down upon.

So what is wise behavior?

First, know how to listen rather than speak.

Wise people do not talk much.

They speak little.

But they speak at the right time.

Those who talk a lot often reveal their weaknesses.

Those who talk less create mystery.

And respect.

Listen more.

Observe more.

And only speak when you have understood clearly.

Second, learn to control your emotions.

Do not let emotions control your behavior.

In an argument…

the one who stays calm is the winner.

In a conflict…

the one who controls their anger is the wise one.

Emotions are good.

But do not let them betray you.

Third, respect everyone.

No matter who they are.

Rich or poor.

High or low.

Whether a CEO or a janitor.

How you treat those below you…

says a lot about who you are.

Respect everyone.

Because every person deserves respect.

Fourth, know how to say thank you and sorry.

Two simple phrases.

Yet the secret of all ages.

Say thank you when someone helps you.

Say sorry when you do wrong.

Do not be afraid to say those two phrases.

They do not make you small.

They make you noble in others' eyes.

Fifth, do not compare and do not judge.

Everyone has their own circumstances.

Their own story.

Their own battle.

You do not know what they have been through.

So do not rush to judge.

And do not compare them to others.

Sixth, know when to be silent.

Silence is sometimes the best answer.

When you do not know what to say…

be silent.

When you are angry…

be silent.

When you are unsure…

be silent.

Silence is not weakness.

It is wisdom.

Seventh, learn to give.

Not material things.

But give your attention.

Give your time.

Give your smile.

Give your encouragement.

Those who know how to give…

are the richest.

Because they understand…

giving is receiving.

And finally…

be yourself.

Do not pretend.

Do not wear a mask.

People will sense your authenticity.

And love you for it.

Wise behavior is not…

saying flowery words.

Or making polite gestures.

It is sincerity.

It is respect.

It is understanding.

When you treat others…

with all your sincerity…

you have already behaved most wisely.

Because life is not a stage.

It is real relationships.

And the wisest person…

is the one who knows how to make others feel valued.

Be that person.

And you will see…

the world open up to you.

In a beautiful way.
