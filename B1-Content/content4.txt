What tires people the most… Is not work, but overthinking.

What exhausts people the most…

is often not the amount of work.

It is…

the thoughts that never stop.

A tired body…

can rest.

But a mind always full of worry…

often does not know how to rest.

People spend a lot of time…

thinking about things that have not happened yet.

Worrying about the future.

Regretting the past.

Analyzing a single comment.

Interpreting an attitude.

Imagining dozens of scenarios that never existed.

And then…

we exhaust ourselves.

Thinking is not bad in itself.

It helps people be more careful.

More thoughtful.

But when thinking goes beyond a limit…

it begins to take away peace.

Not every problem…

is solved by thinking more.

Not everything…

needs to be controlled.

There are things…

the more you hold in your mind…

the heavier they become.

Life already has enough pressure.

What people need…

is not more thinking.

It is learning to put down what cannot be changed.

Learning to stop.

Learning to let the mind breathe.

Because growing up is not only about taking responsibility.

It is also knowing…

when to stop making things hard for yourself.

A peaceful mind…

is not a mind without problems.

It is a mind that knows what is worth keeping…

and what should be let go.
