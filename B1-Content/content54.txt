An entrepreneurial mindset starts small.

You don't need a million dollars.

To start a business.

You don't need a great idea.

To create a company.

You only need…

to start small.

That is the true entrepreneurial mindset.

Not waiting for perfect conditions.

But making use of what you have.

Every big company…

started from something very small.

Apple started in a garage.

Amazon started as an online bookstore.

Facebook started in a dorm room.

No one started at the top.

They started at the bottom.

And climbed step by step.

An entrepreneurial mindset…

first of all, is an action mindset.

Many people wait.

Wait until they have enough money.

Wait until they have enough time.

Wait until they have enough knowledge.

But waiting is endless.

The perfect time will never come.

So start now.

With what you have.

Where you stand.

Second…

is an experimental mindset.

You don't have to be right from the start.

Try. If wrong, fix it.

Entrepreneurship is like doing experiments.

You form a hypothesis.

You test it.

You measure the results.

You adjust.

There is no failure.

Only lessons.

Third…

is a value-focused mindset.

Don't ask how to make money.

Ask how to create value.

Money is the result of value.

Not the goal.

If you solve someone else's problem…

money will come naturally.

Find a small problem.

A problem you can solve.

Start from there.

Fourth…

is a lean mindset.

It doesn't have to be perfect.

Just good enough to start.

Create the first version quickly.

A minimum viable product.

And improve gradually.

Don't wait until it's perfect to launch.

Because that day will never come.

Fifth…

is a persistent mindset.

Most startups fail.

Not because of a bad idea.

But because they give up too soon.

The entrepreneurial path is full of thorns.

There will be days you want to quit.

But remember why you started.

Persist.

But don't be stubborn.

Know when to pivot.

Know when to change.

Starting from small things…

doesn't mean thinking small.

It means starting with small steps.

To reach big goals.

Start.

Even if just one step today.

Tomorrow will be another step.

And one day…

when you look back…

you will be amazed at how far you've come.

From the smallest of things.
