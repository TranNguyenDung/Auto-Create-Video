Learning to love yourself.

There is one most important love…

that many people forget.

It is the love for yourself.

Do you love yourself?

Not in a selfish way.

But deeply and sincerely.

Accepting your true self.

With all its imperfections.

Many people think…

loving yourself is arrogance.

Is being self-centered.

But no.

Loving yourself is the foundation…

for loving others.

You cannot give what you do not have.

If you do not love yourself…

how can you know how to love others?

Learning to love yourself…

begins with forgiving yourself.

Forgiving your past mistakes.

Forgiving the times you were not good enough.

Forgiving wrong decisions.

You did the best you could…

with what you had at that time.

That is enough.

Next…

stop comparing.

Comparison is the thief of joy.

When you compare yourself to others…

you are hurting yourself.

You are denying your own journey.

Everyone has their own path.

Focus on your path.

Take care of yourself.

The way you take care of someone you love.

Eat well.

Sleep enough.

Exercise.

Rest when needed.

Do not wait until you are exhausted…

to realize you need rest.

Say no when needed.

Set boundaries.

Because boundaries are part of love.

When you say no to others…

you are saying yes to yourself.

That is not selfish.

That is necessary.

Learning to love yourself…

is also learning to listen to your body.

Listen to your emotions.

When you are sad, allow yourself to be sad.

When you are tired, allow yourself to rest.

Do not dismiss your feelings.

They are signals.

Telling you something is not right.

Talk to yourself…

with a loving voice.

Instead of "why are you so stupid"…

say "it's okay, everyone makes mistakes".

Instead of "you are so ugly"…

say "you have your own unique beauty".

The language you use with yourself…

is the most powerful language.

Because it shapes how you see yourself.

A small exercise each evening…

write down three things you love about yourself.

It could be your personality.

It could be your actions.

It could be your appearance.

Write every day.

And you will see…

that list grows longer each time.

Because you are already lovable.

You just need to realize it.

Loving yourself…

is not a destination.

It is a journey.

Every day you take one more step.

Closer to your true self.

And when you can love yourself…

you will find life lighter.

Because you no longer need someone else…

to fill the emptiness in your heart.

You are already complete within.

And that is the only love…

that will never leave you.
