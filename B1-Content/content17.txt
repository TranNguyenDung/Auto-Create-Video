The art of listening.

Have you ever…

truly listened to someone?

Not listening with your ears.

But listening with your whole heart.

People often speak…

but few know how to listen.

We listen to reply.

Not to understand.

When someone is speaking…

you are usually preparing what you are going to say.

You are not really there.

You are inside your own head.

Listening is an art.

And also a gift.

The gift of presence.

When you truly listen to someone…

you are telling them…

you matter.

You are valuable.

I am here for you.

There is a story about a boy…

who went to a wise man and said…

teacher, I have no friends.

The wise man looked at him and asked…

do you know how to listen?

The boy was surprised…

what is so hard about listening?

The wise man smiled…

then go home and practice listening…

for one month, then come back.

A month later the boy returned…

and said I have practiced listening…

but I still have no friends.

The wise man said…

you have only practiced listening with your ears.

Now practice listening with your heart.

Listening is not about staying silent.

It is about letting the other person…

feel understood.

When someone shares their sorrow…

do not rush to give advice.

Do not rush to say "I understand".

Because you cannot fully understand.

Just be there.

Just stay quiet.

Just hold their hand.

Sometimes silence…

is the deepest answer.

Listening requires patience.

It requires you to set aside yourself.

Set aside your judgments.

Set aside your biases.

And simply be present.

In this noisy world…

everyone wants to talk.

Everyone wants to be heard.

But those who know how to listen…

are the rarest of all.

Did you know…

on average, a person spends 60% of their time…

talking about themselves?

We love…

the feeling of sharing.

Of telling our stories.

But if everyone only speaks…

then who will listen?

Practice listening every day.

Start by asking questions.

And then be silent.

Truly silent…

to hear the answer.

Do not interrupt.

Do not assume.

Do not prepare a response.

Just listen.

You will be surprised…

by what you discover.

Because each person is a whole world.

With untold stories.

Unspoken pains.

Unfulfilled dreams.

And you have the honor…

of entering that world.

If you know how to listen.

Listening is the art of love.

The foundation of every relationship.

The key to understanding.

Learn to listen…

not only with your ears…

but with your whole heart.

And you will see…

the world becomes different.

No longer just noisy sound.

But symphonies…

of souls speaking out.
